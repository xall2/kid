Summer Camp

Our Summer 2022/2023 Study Camps are intensive holiday programmes aimed at Early Years, for parents who want their kids to enjoy winter within a safe, fun and supportive environment. Students study a different topic each week at this kids winter camp in our kindergarten, using a unique approach of hands-on experiential learning to ignite interest and improve not only their English skills but their knowledge and awareness of the world around them. Each week is a complete, stand-alone camp. Students can register for as many camps as they wish. Our Study Camps are suitable for all levels from beginners to advanced learners. A new camp begins every Monday.
